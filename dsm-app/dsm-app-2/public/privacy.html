DSM — Privacy Policy

DiLorenzo Soccer Mindset (“DSM”, “we”, “us”). Last updated: June 3, 2026.

DSM is a mental-performance app for competitive youth soccer players. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. Questions: valentino@dilorenzosoccermindset.com.

What we collect

  • Account: email address, name.
  • Profile: age, playing position, club/team, league, country, identity goal, and onboarding answers you provide.
  • Activity you create: action-step logs, weekly check-ins, match logs, habit tracking, ball-mastery logs, badges/XP, voice-journal recordings and their transcripts, and your chat messages with Coach V.

We do not sell your data, show third-party ads, or use your data to train external advertising profiles.

Why we collect it

To run the app’s features (coaching, tracking, progression, leaderboards) and to send you a weekly recap email. AI coaching responses are generated from the context you provide.

Where it’s stored

Your data is stored in a private Supabase (PostgreSQL) database hosted in the United States, protected by row-level security so each user can only access their own records.

Who can see your data

  • You — all of your own data.
  • A linked parent — a sanitized, read-only view (streak, recent matches, focus area). Parents cannot see your private chats with Coach V.
  • Your coach and the app administrator — for coaching and support.

Third-party processors

We share data only with the service providers needed to operate the app:

  • Supabase — database & authentication
  • Anthropic (Claude) — generates Coach V responses from the conversation context you send
  • Resend — transactional & recap email
  • Vercel — application hosting
  • Google Drive — hosting of course video lessons

Children’s privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

The app is intended for athletes aged 13 and older. If a user is under 13, we require verifiable parental consent before any data is collected, and the account remains paused until a parent approves. Parents may review or request deletion of their child’s data at any time by emailing us. We collect only the data needed for the app to function.

Your rights & choices

  • Access / export: export all of your data from the app at More → Settings → Download my data.
  • Deletion: permanently delete your account and all associated data from More → Settings → Delete my account, or by emailing us. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, your records are removed from our database immediately via cascading deletion.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the “last updated” date above.
